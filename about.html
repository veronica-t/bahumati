About Us

Bahumati (n. bahu-mathi), offers unique presents at an affordable price. Exchanging gifts is a symbol of affection and has always been exciting for both guests and hosts on special occasions and festivals. Many traditional events include giving a party favor bag to every visitor who attends the ceremonies. Bahumati offers many return gift options that suit you. We strive to deliver a variety of hand-picked selections of traditional Indian gifts and handicrafts delivered from artisans to your doorstep. We provide the widest platform for handicraft items to assist local craftsmen in the pursuit of business. Lounge andexperience stress-free shopping with us, where gifting is made easy in just a few clicks!
